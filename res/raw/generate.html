Generate

Start the calculation and visualization of the Ljapunow fractal according to the current parameter settings by clicking on the Generate item in the options menu.

The result of the calculation is updated on screen every half a second. Depending on the number of iterations it may take from a few seconds to several minutes or even much longer until the fractal is calculated.

Settings changed while a calculation is running do not affect that calculation but every following.

Stop

After a fractal calculation has been started it can be stopped at any time by either clicking on the status bar in the middle of the screen or choosing the Stop item from the options menu.
