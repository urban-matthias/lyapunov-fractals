Drag

Single-touch gesture: Click on screen and then move along x-axis and y-axis.

Zoom

Single-touch gesture: Double-click on screen and then move up to zoom in, and down to zoom out.

Multi-touch gesture: Click with two fingers on screen and pinch to zoom out, and reverse pinch to zoom in.

Repeat last action

Single-touch gesture: Click long on screen and choose the listed action to repeat it.
